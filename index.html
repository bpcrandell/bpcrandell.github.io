Case studies

Problem → what I did → what changed.

FLAGSHIP · INSURTECH

Enterprise AI Reporting Platform

PROBLEM
Dozens of legacy BI reports and spreadsheets scattered across the business — no single source of truth, no consistent access control, analysts fielding the same questions daily.
BUILT
An SSO-secured, self-serve platform with config-driven publishing: register a report and the pipeline generates the Python refresh job, the React route, and the access-control registry. Embedded an AI chat copilot on a modular skill framework so non-technical users query the data in plain English.
RESULT
A sprawl of legacy reports became one trusted platform serving 200+ people across the company, every migrated metric validated against source before cutover. Replacing per-seat commercial BI licenses with the company's own SSO-protected suite cut roughly $35K a year in software costs — and new reports now ship through one repeatable path instead of one-off builds that used to run into weeks.

STRATEGIC INITIATIVE · PROFITABILITY

A Lead-Source Change Worth $120K a Year

PROBLEM
Consumers moving through the lead funnel weren't always landing on the best-fit outcome — leaving profit on the table at the exact moment they convert.
BUILT
Dug into the funnel to find where consumer behavior could be steered toward better-fit matches, then partnered with the business to change how leads flow through the source — turning a data insight into an operational change, not just a chart.
RESULT
~$120K in additional profit a year through the lead source — a recurring, year-over-year gain that stacks rather than a one-time bump, by improving the fit between consumers and outcomes instead of only reporting on it.
Funnel analysisConsumer behaviorProfit optimizationLead strategyRecurring impactBusiness partnership
WEB PORTAL · AUTOMOTIVE

Dealer Network Data Portal

PROBLEM
A major automaker's nationwide dealer network needed a secure way to submit their own data, plus reliable dashboards for enrollments, onboarding, and lead audits.
BUILT
A secure self-serve upload portal with the CRM integrations behind it, plus Power BI dashboards on SQL Server — requirements through data modeling through refresh debugging.
RESULT
